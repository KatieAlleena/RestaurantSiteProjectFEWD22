Meet our Head Chef: Link!

Link served as a Knight of Hyrule before the Calamity just over 100 years ago.

On his journey to rescue Princess Zelda, he learned everything he could about wild food and cooking with local forage.

This restaurant has been hand-built here in Hateno village, where Link not only cooks for his neighbors and any wandering adventurers, but also teaches new chefs how to use sustainably-sourced ingredients in all of their cooking.

Occasionally, there will be events in Tarrey Town and Lurelin Village in which a pop-up version of this restaurant will be featured. Check our directions page for event information!
